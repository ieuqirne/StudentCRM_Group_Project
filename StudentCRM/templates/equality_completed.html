Note to Learning Providers:

Please read the document for learning providers on how to use the Equality Monitoring Form”.

This form must be printed out and completed by the participant.

You must ensure that:

  • All sections of this SDS Graduate Apprenticeship Equality Monitoring Form are given to the participant before asking the participant any of the questions in Sections A and B
  • The participant completes section A
  • The learning provider returns Section A to SDS
  • The participant reads and signs the form in Section B
  • The learning provider returns the completed Section B to SDS

Section A of this form must be securely disposed of immediately once the information has been sent to Skills Development Scotland.

Section B must be retained at all times by the learning provider for inspection, and disposed of in accordance with such other instructions as SDS shall provide.

Section A

SDS Privacy Notice

The Skills Development Scotland Co Ltd (“SDS”, “we” or “us”) is the national skills body supporting the people and businesses of Scotland to develop and apply their skills.

The Graduate Apprenticeship Project

The Graduate Apprenticeship project (“GA Project”) is a project funded by SDS to support the training of individuals (“participants”, “you” or ”your”), and which is operated and delivered by Universities, Colleges or other educational or training providers (“training partners”) and funded through SDS by the Scottish Government and the European Social Fund.

SDS does not operate or deliver the GA Project, but we do assist the Scottish Government monitor and report on its delivery, quality and outcomes, as well as assisting in the monitoring of equalities data.

In doing so, SDS will collect and process the information about you. SDS has set out details of how SDS will use your personal data in SDS’s Graduate Apprentice Privacy Notice. Your training provider must provide you with a copy of the Privacy Notice and give you an opportunity to read it, before you complete and sign this form.
